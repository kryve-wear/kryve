Store Policies

Everything you need to know about our returns, refunds, and shipping.

🔄 Kryve Returns & Refunds Policy

Every item at KRYVE is made-to-order, created just for you when you purchase it. Because of that, we can’t accept returns like a traditional clothing store. But don’t worry — if there’s ever an issue with your order, we’ve got you covered.

✅ When We Accept Returns/Refunds

  • Your order arrived damaged or misprinted
  • You received the wrong item, color, or size (our mistake)

👉 Contact us at support@kryve.com within 14 days of delivery with a photo of the issue.

❌ When We Can’t Accept Returns

  • Wrong size ordered (please check our size chart before purchasing)
  • Change of mind or style preference

🕒 How Long Refunds Take

If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take 5–10 business days to appear.

🔁 Exchanges

We don’t currently offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or style, please place a new order.

❓ Questions

If you have any concerns, email kryvewear@gmail.com — we’re here to help.

🚚 Kryve Shipping Policy

At KRYVE, every product is made just for you when you order it. Because of that, shipping works a little differently compared to big warehouses. Here’s everything you need to know 👇

🌍 Where We Ship

We ship worldwide. Your order will be sent from the nearest partner facility (U.S., Canada, or Europe) whenever possible. If a local facility can’t fulfill the item, it may come from another country.

💰 Shipping Costs

Shipping is charged per product, since items are often made and shipped separately.

  • USA (domestic): Hoodies & Sweatpants → $6 each; T-Shirts, Caps & Accessories → $4.50 each
  • Canada (domestic): Hoodies & Sweatpants → $8 each; T-Shirts, Caps & Accessories → $5.50 each
  • International (outside US/CA): Hoodies & Sweatpants → $12 each; T-Shirts, Caps & Accessories → $7 each

🆓 Free Shipping

  • USA & Canada: Free shipping on all orders over $60
  • Worldwide: Free shipping on all orders over $100

👉 Tip: Add an extra item to your cart to unlock free shipping.

⏱ Delivery Times

  • USA: 5–7 business days
  • Canada: 6–9 business days
  • International: 8–12 business days

Delivery times may vary depending on customs, shipping carrier, and exact location.

🔄 Split Orders

If you order multiple items, they may arrive in separate packages (since they can be made in different facilities).

❓ Questions

If you have any shipping questions, email us at kryvewear@gmail.com and we’ll be happy to help.
